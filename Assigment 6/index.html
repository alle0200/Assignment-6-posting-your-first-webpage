Terry the Tyrannosaurus

Once upon a time, there was a T-Rex named Terry. Terry was a very cool T-Rex. Everyone could tell that Terry was cool because he wore a pair of sunglasses everywhere. That's just what cool people do; they wear sunglasses all the time.

One day, Terry ran into Lightning McQueen on the side of the road. Lightning McQueen said hi to Terry, but Terry could not say hi back because he was a dinosaur (and everyone knows that dinosaurs can't talk). Lightning McQueen took great offense to this and started blowing things up.

"You've yee'd your last haw, partner," Lightning McQueen grumbled as fire engulfed the vicinity.

Terry would have responded to this, but he didn't because he was a dinosaur (and dinosaurs can't talk). Instead, Terry just stood in front of the explosions and looked really cool. The blasts swallowed Terry and Lightning McQueen whole, resulting in a firey death for both of them. There were no survivors, but at least Terry looked really cool for 5 seconds.

The End.
